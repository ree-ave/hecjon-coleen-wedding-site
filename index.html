Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring someone else to your wedding with me?

We would love to welcome more guests, but we hope you understand that we are keeping our celebration intimate, with each seat reserved for a specific guest.

What should we do after the ceremony?

Please enjoy our cocktail hour right after the ceremony. A selection of our favorite food carts will be available, and you're welcome to use this time to register, mingle, or visit the photo studio. Our coordinators will be nearby if you need any assistance.

Can we sit anywhere during the reception?

We kindly ask everyone to follow the seating arrangement we've prepared for your comfort. Our coordinators will gladly guide you to your assigned seats.

Can we bring kids?

While we love your little ones, we hope you understand that we will be hosting an adults-only celebration to fully enjoy this special day with you.

When should I RSVP by?
