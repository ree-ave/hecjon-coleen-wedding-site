Our Story

He was a lost, wandering young man — searching for direction, for meaning, for a place where his heart could finally rest. She was a girl doing her best to keep everything together, trying to make sense of life while quietly carrying more than anyone ever knew.

And then, in the most unexpected moment, their worlds met.

He found her at her lowest when life felt heavier than usual. Instead of walking away, he stayed. Piece by piece, he helped pick up what was broken, not to fix her, but to show her she never had to carry anything alone again. And in loving him, she helped him find the clarity, strength, and purpose he had long been searching for.

What started as two weary souls crossing paths soon blossomed into something beautiful. They became college sweethearts — nursing students who studied side by side, survived exams together, and held on to the same dream: to become better people each day, instruments of healing, angels with stethoscopes.

They grew as individuals, but even more as partners. Through quiet nights, long shifts, failures, victories, and whispered prayers, they learned what real companionship felt like — steady, patient, and full of grace.

Now, after years of dreaming and striving hand in hand, they are tying the knot. Stepping into forever, still side by side, still choosing each other. Still anchored in faith, still in pursuit of God's purpose for their lives — not as two separate journeys, but finally as one.

This is their story — unexpected, imperfect, redeemed, and beautifully theirs. And it's only the beginning. ❤

Frequently Asked Questions

CAN I BRING SOMEONE ELSE TO YOUR WEDDING WITH ME?

We would love to welcome more guests, but we hope you understand that we are keeping our celebration intimate, with each seat reserved for a specific guest.

WHAT SHOULD WE DO AFTER THE CEREMONY?

Please enjoy our cocktail hour right after the ceremony. A selection of our favorite food carts will be available, and you're welcome to use this time to register, mingle, or visit the photo studio. Our coordinators will be nearby if you need any assistance.

CAN WE SIT ANYWHERE DURING THE RECEPTION?

We kindly ask everyone to follow the seating arrangement we've prepared for your comfort. Our coordinators will gladly guide you to your assigned seats.

CAN WE BRING KIDS?

While we love your little ones, we hope you understand that we will be hosting an adults-only celebration to fully enjoy this special day with you.

WHEN SHOULD I RSVP BY?

Please send your RSVP as soon as you can. The latest we can accept is January 15, 2026.

If we don't receive a response by then, it will be marked "NO." We'll miss you, but we must finalize our guest count with our suppliers. Thank you for understanding.
